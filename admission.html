ADMISSIONS PROCEDURE

Bring to the table win-win survival strategies to ensure proactive domination. At the end of the day, going forward,

  • Admissions are now open to all children solely depending upon the age and according to availability of seats.
  • However, the right of admission is reserved. Every candidate must be introduced by the parent / guardian in person who will be responsible for the good conduct of the candidate..
  • Application for fresh admission to Nursery, LKG, UKG and Standard I, for the academic year commencing in June, will start from December of the previous year. A copy of birth certificate should be produced along with the application form.
  • Candidates seeking admission from other institutions should pass a written test conducted by the school...
  • Application for admission should be made in the prescribed form duly accompanied by a Transfer certificate issued by the last school attended. ..
  • The prescibed admission fee once paid will not be refunded under any circumstances. ..
1 Birth Certificate from the Municipal Corporation / Civic Authorities
2 A copy of the latest progress report certified by the school in which the student last studied ( if applicable )
3 2 individual passport size color photographs of the students and the parents to be enclosed
4 Transfer Certificate / Migration Certificate
5 Wellness Record duly signed by the physician
The student and both the parents should be present at the time of admission.
